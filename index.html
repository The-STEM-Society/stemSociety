What We Do

The STEM Society empowers high school students to excel in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, with a focus on championing women in STEM. We offer workshops led by female leaders, tailored career counselling, and access to valuable resources. Engage with our podcast for insights from professionals, benefit from study support, and explore research opportunities. Join us in promoting inclusivity and shaping the future of STEM.



We Offer

  • Women in STEM Advocacy: Workshops and initiatives led by inspiring female leaders
  • Tailored Career Counselling: One-on-one guidance and exclusive webinars
  • Inspiring Podcast episodes: Insights and career advice from STEM professionals
  • Comprehensive Study Support: Tutoring sessions, exam prep workshops, and notes distribution
  • Research Opportunities: Mentorship programs and events to showcase your work
  • Engaging Blog Content: Weekly articles and a platform for community contributions
